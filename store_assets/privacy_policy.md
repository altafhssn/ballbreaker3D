# Smash Breaker — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 10, 2026
**Developer:** Straw Hat Studio
**Contact:** sameermacmini@gmail.com

## Summary

Smash Breaker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.

## Data we collect

**None.** Smash Breaker:

- Does not require an account or sign-in
- Does not collect personal information (name, email, location, contacts, etc.)
- Does not use analytics or tracking SDKs
- Does not contain advertising
- Does not access the internet at runtime
- Does not access device identifiers, camera, microphone, or storage outside its own sandbox

## Data stored on your device

Your game progress (scores, unlocked levels, star ratings, gem balance, cosmetic purchases made with in-game currency, and audio settings) is saved locally on your device in the app's private storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Children

Smash Breaker is suitable for all ages. Because the game collects no data whatsoever, no data is collected from children.

## Changes to this policy

If a future update adds features that involve data collection (for example, online leaderboards), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the in-app listing will reflect the change.
